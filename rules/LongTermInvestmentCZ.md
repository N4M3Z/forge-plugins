LTIP (`dlouhodobý investiční produkt` / `DIP`) and supplementary pension savings (`doplňkové penzijní spoření`) — tax-advantaged retirement savings sharing a 48 000 CZK aggregate deduction cap under [§15a][1]. LTIP functions like a US Traditional IRA: pre-tax deductions, recapture on early withdrawal. Pension savings is comparable to a 401(k) with government matching.

## Aggregate Cap (§15a)

From 2024, all retirement savings deductions share a **single 48 000 CZK/year ceiling** ([zákon 462/2023 Sb.][2]). This replaces the previous per-product limits. The cap covers LTIP + pension savings + life insurance + long-term care insurance.

## LTIP (Long-Term Investment Product)

Introduced by [zákon 462/2023 Sb.][2], effective January 1, 2024. [MF ČR guidance][1].

| Parameter                  | Value                                                    | Source         |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| Deduction                  | within 48 000 CZK/year aggregate                         | [§15a][3]      |
| Minimum holding period     | 120 months (10 years) OR until age 60, whichever later   | [§15a][3]      |
| Eligible products          | securities accounts, unit funds, ETFs, bonds             | [zákon 462/2023][2] |
| Proof document             | `daňové potvrzení k DIP`                                 |                |
| Employer contribution      | tax-exempt up to 50 000 CZK/year (combined)              | [§6(9p)][4]    |
| Early withdrawal           | `dodanění` — recapture of deductions from last 10 years as §10 income | [§15a][3] |

Contracts must be signed on or after January 1, 2024 to qualify as LTIP.

## Supplementary Pension Savings

Includes legacy `penzijní připojištění` (closed to new contracts since [zákon 427/2011 Sb.][5]).

| Parameter                  | Value                                                    | Source         |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| Deduction                  | within 48 000 CZK/year aggregate                         | [§15a][3]      |
| Deductible threshold       | contributions above 1 700 CZK/month                      | [§15a][3]      |
| State contribution         | max 230 CZK/month at 1 000+ CZK personal                | [zákon 427/2011][5] |
| Proof document             | `výpis z doplňkového penzijního spoření`                 |                |
| Employer contribution      | same 50 000 CZK combined limit as LTIP                   | [§6(9p)][4]    |

Only contributions exceeding 1 700 CZK/month count toward the deduction. The first 1 700 CZK/month (20 400 CZK/year) is excluded. `Státní příspěvek` is not taxable and not part of the deduction.

## Employer Contributions

50 000 CZK/year combined exemption across LTIP + pension savings + life insurance ([§6(9p)][4]). Exempt employer contributions do not appear on the DPFO.

When validating: check LTIP contract date (must be 2024+), verify 120-month/age-60 condition, match `potvrzení` to filing year, check all retirement products against the shared 48 000 CZK aggregate, cross-reference employer contributions against the 50 000 CZK combined exemption, flag mid-year contract changes.
