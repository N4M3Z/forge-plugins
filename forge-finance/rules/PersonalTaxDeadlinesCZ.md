DPFO filing deadlines and correction procedures under [zákon 280/2009 Sb.][1] (`daňový řád`).

| Deadline     | Condition                                                              | Source       |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------ |
| 1 April      | Self-filing (paper or electronic without `datová schránka`)            | [§136(1)][2] |
| 2 May        | Electronic filing via `datová schránka`                                | [§136(2)][2] |
| 1 July       | Filing via `daňový poradce` (`plná moc` filed by 1 April)             | [§136(2)][2] |

Base: "do 3 měsíců po uplynutí zdaňovacího období" ([§136(1)][2]). Weekend/holiday shifts to next business day.

`Opravné přiznání` ([§138][3]): filed BEFORE the deadline. Replaces original entirely. No penalty. Always prefer this path when the deadline has not passed.

`Dodatečné přiznání` ([§141][4]): filed AFTER the deadline. Supplements the original. Required when tax was understated or loss overstated. Deadline: end of month following discovery, max 3 years from original deadline.

| Penalty type                           | Calculation                                            | Source       |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ------------ |
| Late filing (`pokuta`)                 | 0.05%/day after 5-day grace, min 500, max 5%/300K CZK | [§250][5]    |
| Late payment (`úrok z prodlení`)       | repo rate + 8pp daily, forgiven under 1 000 CZK        | [§252][6]    |
| Audit penalty (`penále`)               | 20% of understated tax, only if authority finds it      | [§251][7]    |

Self-correction via `dodatečné přiznání` avoids the 20% audit penalty ([§251][7]). Recommend voluntary correction over waiting.

When advising: check calendar first (`opravné` vs `dodatečné`), compute potential penalties for `dodatečné`, verify `plná moc` is filed for advisor deadline, flag if 3-year assessment period is expiring.
